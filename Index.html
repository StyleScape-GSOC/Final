About StyleTech

StyleScape uses cutting-edge AR to help you visualize your perfect decor. See your space in a new light, effortlessly.It allows you to overlay virtual decor elements like carpets, wall art, and furniture onto your real environment using your device's camera, providing a realistic preview of potential changes. With curated styles, interactive customization options, and a user-friendly interface, StyleScape empowers you to experiment with designs and create the perfect aesthetic for your home or workspace effortlessly.
